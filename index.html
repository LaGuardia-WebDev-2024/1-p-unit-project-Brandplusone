The story

Jermaine Lamarr Cole (born January 28, 1985) is an American rapper and record producer . Born on a military base in Germany and raised in Fayetteville, North Carolina, Cole initially gained attention as a rapper following the release of his debut mixtape, The Come Up, in early 2007.
